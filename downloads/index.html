Verifying Downloads

For security, we recommend verifying the integrity of your downloaded ISO files using the SHA256 checksums provided below:

avian-os-ubuntu.iso: e8a4a01d67a7951c57b69d0d802b5c40a5c5c8a3b8fad4d0f7744a5f9258c8a2
avian-os-lite.iso: 3f7d5b43f2c39c9f40b8c2b0bee059b456a2c8a3b8fad4d0f7744a5f9258c8a2
avian-os-kali.iso: 9a2c8a3b8fad4d0f7744a5f9258c8a2e8a4a01d67a7951c57b69d0d802b5c40a

To verify your download on Linux or macOS, use the following command:

sha256sum avian-os-ubuntu.iso

On Windows, use PowerShell:

Get-FileHash -Algorithm SHA256 avian-os-ubuntu.iso
