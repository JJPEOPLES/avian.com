About Avian OS

Avian OS was created with a simple mission: to provide a lightweight, secure, and user-friendly Linux distribution that anyone can use.

Our team of passionate developers and contributors work tirelessly to improve Avian OS, adding new features and ensuring it remains up-to-date with the latest security patches.

We believe in open source software and the power of community. Avian OS is built on the shoulders of giants, leveraging the incredible work of the Linux community while adding our own unique touches.
